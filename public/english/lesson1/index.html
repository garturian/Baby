English Grammar Lesson



Active  主動語態  and Passive  被動語態  voice:
"Active  主動語態  Voice" means the subject of the sentence is performing the action.
 主動語態表示句子的主語正在執行動作 。
"Passive  被動語態  Voice" means the subject of the sentence is receiving the action.
 被動語態表示句子的主語正在接受動作 。
The Boy killed the cat. - Active
 The cat was killed by the boy. - passive
In the  主動語態  voice, the focus is on "who" or "what" is doing the action.
 在主動語態中 ， 重點在於誰或什麼在執行動作 。
For example: "The chef  廚師  cooks the meal."
 例如 ： 廚師烹飪這道菜 。」
In  被動語態  voice, the focus is on the action itself or the object of the action.
 在被動語態中 ， 重點在於動作本身或動作的對象 。
